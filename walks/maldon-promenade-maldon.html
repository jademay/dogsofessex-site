⚓ Maldon Promenade

4.7 (1 reviews)

Park • Circular • 1hr 10 mins • Mud: Low

📍 Maldon⚓ Estuary views👶 Pushchair friendly☕ Cafés nearby🚻 Excellent facilities

Maldon Promenade Park is one of Essex's most popular dog-friendly destinations, offering wide waterside paths, beautiful estuary views and plenty of nearby cafés and attractions. It's ideal for a relaxed stroll with your dog and makes a great day out for the whole family.

🗺️ The route

Parking & directions. There is plenty of parking in the Promenade Park Car Park and limited spaces in the Promenade Park Coach and Car Park. Or, you could park in the town in Butt Lane Car park or White Horse Lane Car Park.

💡 Local tip: Visit early in the morning for a quieter experience. The promenade can become very busy on sunny weekends, but the estuary views are at their best around high tide.

3.3 miles • 1hr 10 mins • Circular • Waterside paths, open parkland & surfaced promenades
