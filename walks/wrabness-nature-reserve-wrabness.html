🌊 Wrabness Nature Reserve

4.8 (1 reviews)

Coastal • Circular • 30 mins • Mud: Moderate

📍 Wrabness🌊 Estuary views🐦 Wildlife rich🚶 Peaceful trails🎨 A House for Essex

Overlooking the Stour Estuary, Wrabness Nature Reserve offers one of the most peaceful and scenic walks in Essex. Expect sweeping estuary views, abundant wildlife and quiet countryside trails, with plenty of opportunities to slow down and enjoy the landscape with your dog.

🗺️ The route

Parking & directions. There is a car park at the entrance to the reserve. Please note there is a height barrier of 2.1m.

💡 Local tip: Keep an eye out for A House for Essex, the striking landmark designed by Grayson Perry. It's one of Essex's most unusual sights and sits just a short detour from the main walking routes.

1.4 miles • 30 mins • Circular • Estuary paths, open grassland & woodland trails
