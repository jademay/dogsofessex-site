Heybridge Basin

4.8 (2 reviews)

Coastal • Circular • 50 mins • Mud: Moderate

Heybridge Historic basin Estuary views Pushchair friendly Historic lock gates
Save this walk Email this walk Share

One of Essex's most popular waterside walks, Heybridge Basin combines peaceful canal paths, estuary views and historic lock gates with several excellent dog-friendly cafés and pubs. It's an easy, mostly flat walk that's suitable for all ages and ideal for a relaxed morning or afternoon with your dog.

Local tip: Arrive early on sunny weekends to make parking easier and enjoy a quieter walk. If the tide is in, the estuary views are particularly beautiful.

Getting there

Parking & directions. Daisy Meadow Car Park is a large and easy to navigate car park right next to the basin - you can pay via the RingGo app or with your credit/debit card at the machines. You can also park at the two nearby pubs if you are planning to eat there; see The Jolly Sailor and The Old Ship in the Make a Day of It section below.
