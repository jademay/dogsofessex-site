Langdon Hills Country Park - Westley Heights

4.7 (1 reviews)

Woodland • Circular • 1hr–2hrs • Mud: Moderate

Basildon Excellent off lead Woodland trails Multiple routes Nature rich
Save Email Share

Westley Heights is one of the most popular parts of Langdon Hills Country Park, with extensive woodland, varied walking routes and plenty of room for dogs to explore. The wide trails and natural surroundings make it an excellent everyday dog walk.

Local tip: The further you venture from the main car park, the quieter the trails become. Dogs with reliable recall will find some excellent off-lead opportunities throughout the woodland.
