🌳 High Woods Country Park

4.6 (1 reviews)

Woodland • Multiple routes • 1hr 30 mins–3hrs • Mud: Moderate

📍 Colchester🐕 Mostly off lead🚗 Free parking🌳 Woodland & lakes👶 Pushchair friendly

High Woods Country Park is one of Colchester's most popular dog walks, combining woodland trails, wildlife lakes and open parkland. With wide paths, plenty of variety and routes suitable for most abilities, it's an excellent choice for everything from a quick weekday stroll to a longer weekend adventure.

🗺️ The route

High Woods Small Circular

Distance 3 miles Time 1hr 30 mins

High Woods Large Circular

Distance 6 miles Time 3hrs

Parking & directions. There are two main car parks: the Turner Road car park (paid, near the Visitor Centre) and the Chanterelle car park (free, located on the High Woods residential estate).

💡 Local tip: Water-loving dogs may be disappointed here. The lakes are a great feature of the park, but dogs aren't allowed to swim in them, so don't forget to bring water on warmer days.
