Epping Forest

Covering thousands of acres, Epping Forest is one of the most iconic dog walking destinations in the UK. With ancient woodland, open glades and countless trails to explore, it's a place where no two walks ever feel quite the same.

Getting there

Parking & directions. Epping Forest has multiple Car Parks, so it is best to search near the part of the forest that you would like to visit.

Local tip: Don't worry about sticking to a single route. The best part of Epping Forest is simply exploring. If your dog enjoys off-lead freedom, some of the quieter woodland tracks away from the main car parks offer miles of adventure.
