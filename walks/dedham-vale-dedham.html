Dedham Vale

5 (1 reviews)

Riverside • Multiple routes • 1hr 40 mins–3hrs 20 mins • Mud: Moderate

Parking charges Limited free parking River to swim
Save this walk Email this walk Share

Dedham Vale offers a beautiful mix of riverside paths, open countryside and dog-friendly trails. With plenty of space to explore and some of Essex's most celebrated views, it's a fantastic choice for a relaxing walk with your dog.

Local tip: Many dogs head straight for the river, so a towel in the car is a good idea, especially after rain when the riverside paths can be muddy.
