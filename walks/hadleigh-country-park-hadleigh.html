🌄 Hadleigh Country Park

4.9 (1 reviews)

Countryside • Multiple routes • 1hr 30 mins–3hrs 30 mins • Mud: Moderate

📍 Hadleigh🌄 Estuary views🐕 Excellent off lead🚗 Free parking🌿 Open countryside

Hadleigh Country Park offers some of the most impressive views in Essex, with rolling hills overlooking the Thames Estuary. The combination of open countryside, varied terrain and huge skies makes it a fantastic destination for adventurous dogs and owners.

Parking & directions. Hadleigh Country Park has a pay and stay car park.

💡 Local tip: The best views are found on the higher ground overlooking the estuary. Bring water for your dog on warm days, as much of the park is exposed with very little shade.
