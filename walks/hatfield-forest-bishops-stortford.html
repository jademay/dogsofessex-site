Hatfield Forest

4.9 (1 reviews)

Woodland • Multiple routes • 40 mins–2hrs 45 mins • Mud: Moderate

Bishop's Stortford Ancient woodland Mostly off lead Lakeside walks National Trust

Hatfield Forest is one of the finest woodland walks in Essex, combining ancient trees, open grassland and peaceful lakeside trails. Managed by the National Trust, it offers a fantastic mix of scenery and space, making it a favourite for both dogs and their owners.

Getting there

Parking & directions. Free parking for National Trust members, please remember your card. Follow the 'P' signs to two large grass car parks and a central access-friendly tarmacked car park near facilities. Exit Gate is locked at Dusk. Smaller Entrance Car Park is open until 4pm.

Local tip: The main lake is beautiful, but don't stop there. Some of the quietest and most scenic sections of Hatfield Forest can be found by following the trails deeper into the woodland away from the visitor centre.
