🌳 Great Notley Country Park

4.7 (1 reviews)

Park • Circular • 50 mins • Mud: Low

📍 Braintree🐕 Mostly off lead🚗 Free parking👶 Pushchair friendly🌳 Open parkland

Great Notley Country Park is one of Essex's most accessible dog walks, with wide paths, open spaces and plenty of route options. Whether you're looking for a quick everyday walk or a longer outing, there's plenty of room for dogs to stretch their legs.

🗺️ The route

Parking & directions. There is a paid for car park onsite.

💡 Local tip: If your dog enjoys a good run, head away from the play areas and visitor facilities towards the larger open meadows. There's plenty of space to explore and some of the quietest sections of the park can be found further from the main entrance.

2 miles • 50 mins • Circular • Wide parkland paths, lakeside trails & open grassland
