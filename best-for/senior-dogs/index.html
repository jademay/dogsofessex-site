How we rate walks for senior dogs

Our Senior Dogs rating weighs distance, terrain difficulty, steep gradients, rest stops and surface quality.

★★★★★ Excellent — Short, flat routes with resting opportunities.
★★★★☆ Good — Suitable for most older dogs.
★★★☆☆ Moderate — Longer distances or uneven terrain.
★★☆☆☆ Limited — May be tiring for some senior dogs.
★☆☆☆☆ Not recommended — Demanding routes unsuitable for most senior dogs.
