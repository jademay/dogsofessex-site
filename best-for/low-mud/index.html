Maldon Promenade
Park

Maldon Promenade

3.3 miles

Maldon Promenade Park is one of Essex's most popular dog-friendly destinations, offering wide waterside paths, beautiful estuary views and plenty of nearby cafés and attractions. It's ideal for a relaxed stroll with your dog and makes a great day out for the whole family.
